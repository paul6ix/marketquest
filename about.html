Our Proprietary Tools

Deep Quest

• A Qualitative tool designed to unearth the sub-conscious disposition of the consumer using projective techniques.
• Methodology relies more on pictures than words to elicit meanings and response (from the consumer/respondent) that are pertinent to the brand and the study objectives.
• We recognize that most of what influences what we do or say; and our purchase decisions occur below the level of awareness
• That’s why we have developed a methodology that unravels what consumers don’t know they know to assist our strategic partners achieve results. We call it “Deep Quest®”
.

Field Quest

• A diagnostic and direct exploratory model that makes for early detection and identification of budding brand issues and thought starters before they become mainstream
• Also relevant in the initial phases of testing to gauge a sample’s expectations, attitudes, and initial interest in a potential product or concept. Stimuli are not utilised, respondents give open-ended, unaided feedback which can be used as building blocks for further testing and validation.
• Field Quest may help identify brand problems when still in a preliminary stage and when issues are fresh
• A flexible platform that sets the stage for a more structured research design, haven identified preliminary issues. Helps generate a formal hypothesis .
