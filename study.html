Pricing study

Market Quest captures the market matrix evaluating key factors like the competitive landscape, purchase behaviour and peculiarity of the product or service. Our methodology finds out what customers like and more importantly, what they are willing to pay and so what the optimum price point is to maximise profit or revenue or market share. Market Quest conducts pricing studies to help clients determine the most profitable price for their product or service.

Image Study

Evaluates the overall impression, opinion, perception and awareness of your publics on your corporate outlook. Market Quest Image studies also evaluates brand image and perception from a consumer’s view point, this however usually forms part of a brand health research. Market Quest does this assessment vis-à-vis competition or other industry players. In most cases a combination of qualitative and quantitative approach is used with the qualitative first digging up salient and pertinent issues and the quantitative validates and gives a measured analysis of findings.
