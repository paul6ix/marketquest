Dan Esiekpe

Highly versatile and intensively creative, Dan had amassed an enviable wealth of experience in the world of branding, marketing and advertising. Previously Marketing Director with Unilever and Nigerian Breweries (The Heineken subsidiary in Nigeria). His career spans decades working in various executive capacities with Heineken and Unilever, in diverse markets and geographies.
Dan is a Brands leader in West Africa, highly regarded as an experienced brands specialist. Practical, logical and strategic in his approach to brands, his foundation stems from a thorough understanding of marketing and advertising from both the agency and client perspective. His footprints are global and indelible with successful stints at Lintas Lagos, Ghana Breweries, Hindustan levers, India; Sierra Leone Breweries, Heineken International Amsterdam and Nigerian Breweries.
A graduate of Mass Communication from the University of Lagos, with additional qualifications from Harvard Business School and The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. Dan is also a Fellow of the Nigerian Institute of Marketing, and an author of two novels, ‘‘The Union of Manifesto” and “Return to Freetown”
